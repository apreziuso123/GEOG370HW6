Homework 6: Census data choropleth

Antonio Preziuso

For this homework, I utilized U.S. Census Bureau data to create a choropleth map that depicts the percent of the population without health insurance by county in Kansas. I created 5 classes and used whole number percentages to create a clear gradient on my map. Furthermore, I used purple as the color to avoid political affiliations with my map.
